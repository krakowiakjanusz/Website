Welcome to the unofficial website of the RGU Music-Enjoying for Those Hard-of-Hearing Society. Here you will find everything you need to know about us.

We're happy to have you here!

At METH we are committed to providing a safe and comfortable environment where people who are hard-of-hearing can relax and enjoy listening to music.
We provide accessible sound systems that amplify the level at which music can be felt through the body, reducing the need for our sense of hearing.
